About the
Photographer

Elena Moreau is a Paris-based photographer whose work explores the intersection of light, architecture, and human presence. With over a decade of experience in editorial and fine art photography, her images have graced the pages of leading publications worldwide.

Her approach combines classical composition with a contemporary sensibility — creating images that feel at once timeless and immediate. She is drawn to quiet moments, negative space, and the poetry found in everyday geometry.
